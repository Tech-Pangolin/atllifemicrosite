EBLF 2025

Enterprise Updates

In December 2024, AM Best assigned Atlanta Life Insurance Company (ALIC) a Financial Strength Rating of B++ (Good) and a Long-Term Issuer Credit Rating of “bbb” (Good), both with a stable outlook. These ratings reflect ALIC's very strong balance sheet, supported by the strongest level of risk-adjusted capitalization as measured by Best's Capital Adequacy Ratio (BCAR). The company's operating performance and enterprise risk management were assessed as marginal, and its business profile as limited, due to its recent re-entry into the group benefits and reinsurance market. In January 2023, Atlanta Life Holdings acquired ALIC and made a capital contribution to support its strategic growth initiatives.

Strategic Focus

Today's employers are facing unprecedented health, productivity, and workforce challenges. Healthcare costs are projected to rise another 8% in 2025 (PwC, 2025), while only 7% of employees feel productive at work (Electro IQ, 2025). At Atlanta Life, we help organizations confront these realities head-on through integrated Health & Productivity solutions that connect employee health, absense management, disability, and return-to-work strategies into a seamless support system.

Our approach ensures employees feel supported at every stage- whether managing chronic conditions, returning after disability, or facing mental health challenges - while helping employers reduce absence, improve engagement, and strengthen organizational performance.
