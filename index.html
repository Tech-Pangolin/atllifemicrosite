What is reinsurance

We Absorb Risk from Insurers for Enhanced Stability

Reinsurance is a risk management strategy used by insurance companies to transfer a portion of their insurance liabilities to other insurers, known as reinsurers. This helps primary insurers mitigate their exposure to large losses and maintain financial stability.

Who We Are

We Empower Communities for Generational Security

Founded in 1905 by Alonzo F. Herndon, a visionary and formerly enslaved man, Atlanta Life Insurance Company (ALIC) is the last remaining Black-founded and owned insurer in the United States. Initially created to help families afford proper burials for their loved ones, ALIC has evolved into a symbol of protection, generational security, and economic empowerment. Today, we proudly continue this legacy as a thriving Group reinsurance carrier, dedicated to providing financial stability and growth for our community.
